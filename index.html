FAQ

Everything you need to know about our blends.

Does Razzel contain artificial coloring?

Never. The vibrant colors of our colas come entirely from the natural fruit extracts and botanicals we use. We strictly adhere to our "No Compromise" philosophy.

Is there caffeine in your drinks?

Yes, our blends contain natural caffeine sourced from green coffee beans to give you that clean, sustained energy boost without the crash.

Where do you ship?

Currently, we ship nationwide across the continental US. We are working hard to expand our international shipping capabilities soon!

Are your cans recyclable?

100%. Sustainability is key to our brand. Our aluminum cans are infinitely recyclable.
